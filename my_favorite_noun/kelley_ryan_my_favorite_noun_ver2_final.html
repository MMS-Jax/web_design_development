Why I Like It

The main reason I enjoy eating Italian food is that I grew up eating Italian food. The flavors and smells from my childhood always trigger happy memories. Cooking was important to everyone in my family and each person had a favorite dish to make. My favorite thing to make was homemade garlic bread. My favorite thing to eat, however, was spaghetti and meatballs that my grandmother would make.

Popular Recipes

There are many popular dishes in Italian cooking. The different regions of Italy all have distinct flavors and ingredients. Here I will list some of most popular dishes and link to some online recipes so you can try them at home!

  1. Spaghetti and Meatballs
  2. Fettucine Alfredo
  3. Tiramisu

I hope you have enjoyed this quick introduction to Italian food. If I could only eat one type for the rest of my life, this would be it without a doubt. Please try some of those recipes linked above if you have never made Italian food. Hopefully, it can bring you the same comfort it brings me. Maybe it can even start a new tradition with you and your family!
